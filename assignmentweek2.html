Welcome to Our 4th Grade Classroom!

We are excited to have you join us this year!

About Me

Hello! My name is Frances Komm, and I am thrilled to be your child’s 4th-grade teacher this year. I have a degree in Elementary Education from Grand Canyon University and have been teaching for 1 year. My passion is fostering a love for learning in my students and helping them achieve their goals.

Classroom Expectations

In our classroom, we value respect, responsibility, and resilience. Students are expected to:

  • Be respectful to peers and teachers.
  • Complete homework and class assignments on time.
  • Participate actively in class discussions.
  • Support one another as a learning community.

Communication with Families

I believe in open communication with families. Throughout the year, I will provide updates through:

  • Monthly newsletters.
  • Email updates regarding important announcements.
  • A class blog where I will post highlights and resources.
  • Regular parent-teacher conferences.

Building Relationships

Creating a strong partnership between families, the school, and myself is essential for student success. I plan to:

  • Host monthly family nights to foster community.
  • Encourage volunteer participation in classroom activities.
  • Share student achievements and progress regularly.
  • Be available for questions or concerns via email and phone.

Volunteer Opportunities

We welcome family involvement in our classroom! Here are some ways you can help:

  • Assist with classroom projects and activities.
  • Chaperone field trips.
  • Read to students or help with reading groups.
  • Organize class parties and celebrations.
  • Share a talent or skill during special presentations.

The Importance of Family Involvement

Family involvement is crucial for enhancing student learning. Research shows that students whose families are engaged in their education perform better academically and socially. Together, we can create a supportive environment that encourages your child’s growth and success!
